Purpose of the Site

The purpose of this website is to inform individuals, both young and old, of the severity and reality of the impact of concussions in the National Football League. We will touch on what exactly a concussion is, how it impacts the human body, and what is currently being done to ensure that steps are being taken to properly raise awareness of the repercussions that concussions can and do have on us as humans.

A Statistical Look into Concussions in the NFL

This page will focus on the actual statistics from the last 10-15 NFL seasons, displaying the number of concussions that have taken place during each season, and will also identify any trends that could be noteworthy for players and fans alike to be aware of.

The literal impact of concussions on the Human Body

This page will examine the human body and will explain in detail what exactly takes place when a concussion occurs. It will include explanations from doctors, all of whom have worked first-hand with former or current NFL players that have dealt with concussion-like symptoms of any kind. They will describe what the risks are of playing in the NFL and what some of the best options are for treating concussions.

Testimonials of Past and Current Players

This page will provide a few testimonials and interviews of a few NFL players (past or current) who have suffered greatly from the effects of concussions. They will describe some of the permanent effects they have been dealing with ever since they were first concussed, and will further shed light on what life has been like ever since they played football professionally.

Raising Awareness

This page will discuss what many current NFL players are actively doing to help educate and raise awareness to people of all ages about the dangers of the nature of football and how it is only becoming more and more of a threat to the health and well-being of humans.
